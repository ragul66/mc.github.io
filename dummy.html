Gandhi Memorial Museum

Gandhi Memorial Museum, established in 1959, is a memorial museum for Mahatma Gandhi located in the city of Madurai in Tamil Nadu, India. Known as Gandhi Museum, it is now one of the five Gandhi Sanghralayas (Gandhi Museums) in the country. It includes a part of the blood-stained garment worn by Gandhi when he was assassinated by Nathuram Godse.

Madurai Bun Parotta Kadai

Bun parotta is a popular Madurai street food that is essentially a cross between a bun and a parotta. It is made by folding the parotta into the shape of a bun, similar to a Malabar parotta. Karupanna and his wife Vijayam claim to have been the first to make bun parotta in 1991 at their stall Madurai Bun Parotta Kada. Bun parotta is typically served with hot curries.
